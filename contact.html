Contact us


Whitethorn are happy to perform at many types of event such as festivals, fetes, WIs and pubs. To book us or to enquire about an event, please contact the side secretary.

We practice every Thursday night in Harrow, north-west London. If you are interested in joining us to dance or play, please contact the secretary.

To contribute photos of us or to talk about the website, please contact the webmaster.
